ThoughtHatchery deck.js template

What is this?

This is ThoughtHatchery template for deck.js. It is designed to be used as the clean and simple template so that our speakers may easily create their slides and share them.

Where to from here?

deck.js is amazingly simple to use. Your slides are simply HTML content and therefore it should be a markup language we all know and interact with regularly. For more details on deck.js please checkout out the website at http://imakewebthings.github.com/deck.js

Why is this so short?

Simple, because deck.js is simple. Beyond, that it is really just here as ThoughtHatchery template and theme so that all the ThoughtHatchery presentations are consistent. For examples I strongly recommend looking at the deck.js Introduction.html for examples of how to create different types of slides.
